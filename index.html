How it works

A gradual sunrise before your alarm

Pick a wake time. Sunrise Alarm starts a slow fade minutes before, ramping your lights (and blinds) from dark to full brightness on the curve you choose. It runs on your Apple Home Hub, so it fires on time even if your phone is away.

Dark The room is still asleep.
Warm glow Light builds gradually.
Full light You wake before the alarm.

Wake time, fade length, and brightness curve are all fully adjustable.

Built for customization

Build a sunrise across your entire room

Most sunrise products are a single dedicated lamp. Sunrise Alarm orchestrates every light you own, in any configuration you choose.

Any number of lights

Add every HomeKit-enabled bulb in your bedroom, or spread the sunrise across multiple rooms. Philips Hue, Nanoleaf, LIFX, Eve, Meross, and anything else in your Apple Home app.

Brightness and brightness curve

Set how bright your lights reach at alarm time. Then choose the curve: a gentle exponential ramp that barely moves at first, or a near-linear fade if you need the room light fast.

Full color and color temperature

Start deep red-orange and shift toward bright daylight white, just like a real sunrise. Works with color-capable bulbs, or color-temperature-only bulbs that shift warm to cool.

On/off switch devices

Have a lamp on a smart plug, or any HomeKit-enabled on/off device? Include it. It switches on at the start of the sunrise window and off after the alarm fires.

Blinds control New

Open your HomeKit-enabled motorized blinds or shades as part of the sunrise sequence. Let real light in alongside your smart lights for the most natural wake-up possible.

Private by design

No accounts, no tracking, no cloud. Your alarms stay on your device and within Apple's HomeKit ecosystem. Nothing leaves your home.

Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an Apple Home Hub?

Yes. An Apple TV (4th generation or later), HomePod, or HomePod mini must be present on your home network. The hub runs the automation locally so your sunrise fires reliably even if your phone is asleep, off, or away. Most HomeKit households already have one. If you don't, the Apple TV HD and HomePod mini are affordable options.

Which devices work with Sunrise Alarm?

Any HomeKit-enabled light, including color bulbs (Philips Hue, Nanoleaf, LIFX) and color-temperature-only bulbs (Eve, Meross, and many others). Smart plugs and on/off switches are supported, so any lamp on a smart plug can join the sunrise. HomeKit-enabled motorized blinds and shades are also supported.

Is this really a one-time purchase?

Yes. You pay $4.99 once on the App Store. There is no subscription and no in-app purchases required.

Is it available on Android?

No. Sunrise Alarm is built exclusively for iOS and Apple HomeKit. Being iOS-only lets us integrate deeply with HomeKit and keep everything on-device.

What data does the app collect?

None. No personal information is collected, stored, or shared. Your alarms and settings stay on your device or within Apple's HomeKit ecosystem. Read the full privacy policy.
