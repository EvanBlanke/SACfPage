Questions

Frequently asked questions

Do I need an Apple Home Hub?

Yes. An Apple TV (4th generation or later), HomePod, or HomePod mini must be present on your home network. The hub runs the automation locally so your sunrise fires reliably even if your phone is asleep, off, or away. Most HomeKit households already have one. If you don't, the Apple TV HD and HomePod mini are affordable options.

Which devices work with Sunrise Alarm?

Any HomeKit-enabled light, including color bulbs and color-temperature-only bulbs (Philips Hue, Nanoleaf, LIFX, Eve, Meross, and many others). Smart plugs and on/off switches are supported, so any lamp on a smart plug can join the sunrise. HomeKit-enabled motorized blinds and shades are also supported.

Is this really a one-time purchase?

Yes. You pay $4.99 once on the App Store. There is no subscription and no in-app purchases required.

Is it available on Android?

No. Sunrise Alarm is built exclusively for iOS and Apple HomeKit. Being iOS-only lets us integrate deeply with HomeKit and keep everything on-device.

What data does the app collect?

None. No personal information is collected, stored, or shared. Your alarms and settings stay on your device or within Apple's HomeKit ecosystem. Read the full privacy policy.

Visit our support page or email us at Support@sunrisealarmapp.com. We read everything and reply personally.
