Before You Email

The answers below cover the most common questions. They may save you a trip to your inbox.

A Home Hub is Required

Sunrise Alarm schedules alarms through Apple HomeKit. For alarms to run reliably (especially while your iPhone is asleep or away from home), you need an Apple Home Hub on the same network. Compatible Home Hubs are: Apple TV, HomePod, or HomePod mini.

Without a Home Hub, automations may not fire. This is an Apple HomeKit requirement, not a limitation of the app.

Supported Devices

Sunrise Alarm works with any HomeKit-enabled device in these categories: lights (color and white), on/off switches, and motorized blinds or shades. If your device appears in the Apple Home app, it will appear in Sunrise Alarm.
